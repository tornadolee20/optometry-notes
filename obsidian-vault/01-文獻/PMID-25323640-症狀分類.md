# Symptomatology associated with accommodative and binocular vision anomalies

> 作者：García-Muñoz Á, Carbonell-Bonete S, Cacho-Martínez P
> 來源：J Optom. 2014;7:178-192

---

## Abstract

### Purpose
To determine the symptoms associated with accommodative and non-strabismic binocular dysfunctions and to assess the methods used to obtain the subjects' symptoms.

### Methods
We conducted a scoping review of articles published between 1988 and 2012 that analysed any aspect of the symptomatology associated with accommodative and non-strabismic binocular dysfunctions. The literature search was performed in Medline (PubMed), CINAHL, PsycINFO and FRANCIS. A total of 657 articles were identified, and 56 met the inclusion criteria.

### Results
We found 267 different ways of naming the symptoms related to these anomalies, which we grouped into 34 symptom categories. Of the 56 studies, 35 employed questionnaires and 21 obtained the symptoms from clinical histories. We found 11 questionnaires, of which only 3 had been validated: the convergence insufficiency symptom survey ([[CISS V-15]]) and CIRS parent version, both specific for convergence insufficiency, and the Conlon survey, developed for visual anomalies in general.

### Conclusions
There is a wide disparity of symptoms related to accommodative and binocular dysfunctions in the scientific literature, most of which are associated with near vision and binocular dysfunctions. The only psychometrically validated questionnaires that we found (n=3) were related to convergence insufficiency and to visual dysfunctions in general and there no specific questionnaires for other anomalies.

## 11 Questionnaires Found

### 已驗證問卷（3種）

#### 1. [[CISS V-15]] (Convergence Insufficiency Symptom Survey)
- **開發者**：Borsting E, 2003
- **對象**：[[CI 診斷標準|CI]]，兒童與成人
- **題數**：15 題（全部近距離視覺相關）
- **量表**：Likert 5 點（0-4）
- **分數範圍**：0-60
- **Cut-off**：≥16 = 有 CI 症狀
- **驗證方法**：ROC analysis

#### 2. Conlon Survey
- **開發者**：Conlon E, 1999
- **對象**：一般視覺異常，成人
- **題數**：23 題
- **量表**：Likert 4 點
- **分數**：Low (0-24), Moderate (25-48), High (49-69)
- **驗證方法**：RASCH analysis

#### 3. CIRS Parent Version
- **開發者**：Borsting E, 1999
- **對象**：CI，兒童（家長填寫）
- **題數**：15 題
- **Cut-off**：≥9 = likely CI

## Discussion 重點

1. **267 種不同表達方式** → 歸類為 34 種症狀
2. 大多數症狀與**近距離視覺**和**雙眼視覺異常**相關
3. 只有 **3 種問卷經過驗證**（CISS V-15、CIRS parent、Conlon）
4. **沒有針對 CI 以外異常的專用問卷**
5. 各種異常的症狀**高度重疊**，難以單靠症狀區分

---

## 臨床應用

- 使用 [[CISS V-15]] 篩檢 CI（≥16 分需進一步檢查）
- 症狀無法區分具體診斷，需要結合客觀檢查
- 大部分症狀與近距離工作相關
