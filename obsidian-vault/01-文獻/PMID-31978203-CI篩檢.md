# ROC curve analysis for CI screening in young adults

> 作者：Byeong-Yeon Moon et al.
> 來源：PLoS One. 2020

---

## Abstract

Convergence insufficiency ([[CI 診斷標準|CI]]) is a dysfunction of binocular vision that is associated with various signs and symptoms in near work. We aimed to evaluate the ability of screening tests to discriminate CI from other binocular vision anomalies and normal binocular vision in young adults.

**Results:**
- Prevalence of CI: 10.3% to 21.2%
- [[NPC]]、[[Sheards Criterion|Sheard's criterion]]、[[Percivals Criterion|Percival's criterion]] showed high discriminative ability
- Sheard's criterion showed highest diagnostic performance for CI with 3 signs

---

## CI 診斷標準（本研究使用）

Presence of sign 1 AND sign 2 or 3:

1. **Exophoria at near ≥4Δ** greater than at distance
2. **PFV at near** <14Δ for blur or <18Δ for break
3. **[[NPC]] ≥7.5 cm**

---

## 所有 NSBVD 診斷標準

### 輻輳異常

#### [[CI 診斷標準|CI (Convergence Insufficiency)]]
1. Near exo ≥4Δ > distance exo
2. PFV near <14Δ blur or <18Δ break
3. NPC ≥7.5 cm

#### [[CE 診斷標準|CE (Convergence Excess)]]
1. Near eso ≥4Δ > distance eso
2. NFV near <11Δ blur or <19Δ break
3. AC/A >5/1

### 開散異常

#### [[DI 診斷標準|DI (Divergence Insufficiency)]]
1. Distance eso ≥4Δ > near eso
2. NFV distance <5Δ break
3. AC/A <3/1

#### [[DE 診斷標準|DE (Divergence Excess)]]
1. Distance exo ≥4Δ > near exo
2. PFV distance <7Δ blur or <15Δ break
3. AC/A >5/1

### 基本斜位

#### [[BX 診斷標準|BX (Basic Exophoria)]]
1. Exo <4Δ difference between distance and near
2. PFV distance <7Δ blur or <15Δ break, AND PFV near <14Δ blur or <18Δ break
3. AC/A 3–5/1

#### [[BE 診斷標準|BE (Basic Esophoria)]]
1. Eso <4Δ difference between distance and near
2. NFV distance <5Δ break AND NFV near <11Δ blur or <19Δ break
3. AC/A 3–5/1

### 調節異常

#### [[AI 診斷標準|AI (Accommodative Insufficiency)]]
1. Reduced AA for minimum = 15.0 - 0.25 × age
2. PRA <1.75 D
3. BAF <13 cpm

#### [[AE 診斷標準|AE (Accommodative Excess)]]
1. NRA <1.75 D
2. BAF <13 cpm

#### [[調節靈活度異常|Accommodative Infacility]]
1. NRA & PRA <1.75 D
2. BAF <13 cpm

### 融像異常

#### [[FVD 診斷標準|FVD (Fusional Vergence Dysfunction)]]
1. Normal phoria (distance 1 exo ±1Δ, near 3 exo ±3Δ)
2. Reduced PFV and NFV at distance and near
3. AC/A 3–5/1

## [[Sheards Criterion|Sheard's Criterion]] 計算

```
Sheard's value = (2/3 × phoria) - (1/3 × compensating fusional vergence)
```

- **> 0**：Vergence anomaly likely（不通過）
- **≤ 0**：Passes criterion（通過）
- 外斜位：用 PFV 作為 compensating vergence
- 內斜位：用 NFV 作為 compensating vergence

**臨床範例：**
- Near phoria: 8 exo
- PFV blur at near: 12Δ
- Sheard's = (2/3 × 8) - (1/3 × 12) = 5.33 - 4 = **1.33**
- 結果：>0，不通過 → symptomatic CI likely

---

## [[Percivals Criterion|Percival's Criterion]] 計算

```
Percival's value = (1/3 × greater vergence) - (2/3 × lesser vergence)
```

- **> 0**：Vergence anomaly likely
- **≤ 0**：Passes criterion
- Phoria 應落在 vergence range 的中間 1/3

---

## Key Findings

1. **[[NPC]] 是最佳診斷工具**
   - AUC 0.842–0.920 = excellent discriminative ability
   - Cut-off: >7.2 cm
   - Sensitivity: 1.00

2. **Sheard's criterion 對 CI with 3 signs 更優**
   - 可區分 CI 與其他 BVA
   - 適用於外斜位

3. **CI 盛行率：10.3% to 21.2%**
   - 高於一般人口研究（1.5%–10.8%）
